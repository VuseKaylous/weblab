About us

Intelligent Communication Systems Laboratory (ICSLab) is a key laboratory of Phenikaa University. ICSLab was established in 2019, and leading by Dr. Minhhuy Le. We are doing researches on Machine Learning/Deep Learning, TinyML, Nondestructive Testing, UWB radar, and Smart Sensing. Our mission is to connect academic researches to real-life applications by innovative approaches with AI as the core technology.
