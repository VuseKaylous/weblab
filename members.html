Professors

Minhhuy Le, PhD

Dr. Minhhuy Le received a BSc degree in Mechatronics at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam in 2009. He got a Ph.D. degree in Control and Instrumentation, Chosun University, Korea, in 2014. From 2019, he is working for the Faculty of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Phenikaa University, Vietnam. His interests are non-destructive testing, UWB radar, signal processing, artificial intelligence, and big data.

Email: huy.leminh@phenikaa-uni.edu.vn

Publications: https://scholar.google.co.kr/citations?user=sUACy8cAAAAJ&hl=en

Van Su Luong, PhD

Dr. Van Su Luong received his Bachelor in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from Hung Yen University of Technology and Education, Vietnam, in 2008. He received his Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, National Kaohsiung University of Applied Sciences, Kaohsiung, Taiwan in 2016. Since 2018, he is an employee of Faculty of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Phenikaa University. His research interests include Fluxgate, Spin valve-GMR and TMR magnetometers, Current sensors, E-compass, Eddy-current detectors (EC-NDE), Magnetic Adaptive Testing (MAT-NDE), CMOS-MEMS sensors, and the other Spintronic devices. Dr. Luong was a member of the IEEE Magnetics Society.

Email: su.luongvan@phenikaa-uni.edu.vn

Publications: https://scholar.google.com.vn/citations?user=ZXnb9YQAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao

Khoa Nguyen Dang, PhD

Dr. Khoa Nguyen Dang received his Ph.D. degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Ulsan, Korea in 2018. Since 2019, he works at the Faculty of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Phenikaa university. His interesting research is modeling systems, control systems, unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV), robotics.
